# Zomato Zone Wise Sales Distribution Analysis

## Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze Zomato's sales performance across different zones and identify the factors influencing business growth. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), uncover regional trends, and make data-driven decisions to improve overall business performance.

## Objectives

* Analyze zone-wise and state-wise sales performance.
* Identify regions with declining sales.
* Measure average order cancellation rates using DAX.
* Evaluate customer and restaurant distribution across regions.
* Analyze delivery time and customer ratings.
* Provide actionable business recommendations based on insights.

## Dashboard Features

* 📊 Zone-wise Sales Analysis
* 🌍 State-wise Sales Distribution
* 📈 Revenue and Sales KPIs
* ❌ Average Cancellation Rate Analysis
* 🚚 Delivery Time Analysis
* ⭐ Customer Rating Insights
* 🍽️ Restaurant & Customer Distribution
* 🔍 Interactive Filters and Drill-through

## Business Insights

* Identified high-performing and low-performing sales zones.
* Discovered regions with higher cancellation rates affecting revenue.
* Compared customer demand and restaurant availability across locations.
* Analyzed operational metrics such as delivery time and customer ratings.
* Highlighted areas requiring strategic improvements to enhance customer satisfaction and sales.

## Recommendations

* Focus marketing campaigns on low-performing regions.
* Reduce order cancellations by improving operational efficiency.
* Optimize delivery routes in regions with longer delivery times.
* Increase restaurant partnerships in high-demand areas.
* Monitor key KPIs regularly to support data-driven business decisions.
